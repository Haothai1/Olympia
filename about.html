About Us

What we do

Olympia is a classroom project designed by CSU Fullerton students Cesar Jasso, Nicholas Reeves, and Hao Thai. Olympia is coded primarily using HTML5, CSS, and Javascript, and was built from the ground up over the course of one semester.

Our Mission

At Olympia, we are dedicated to helping everyone find the workout routine that best fits their own lifestyle. Whether it's the occasional jog, or a week straight of pushing yourself to the limit, we're here to help you become the best version of yourself you can be.
